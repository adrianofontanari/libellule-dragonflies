# Underwater

## Earthquake

deafening vibrations.  
Motionless  
I watch the window,  
remove the painting before the collapse.  
outside a car runs over a broom plant.  
Is it the crushed yellow flower that lifts a fallen  
building?

## The Berry

berry not mulberry  
poison and broken branches.  
The stem becoming tree.  
Life!  
It seems unjust  
It adjusts  
slow dawn.

## I Will Fight

for new branches, beehive and sun.

## I Beg You, Go

In the garden  
I collapse,  
no heartbeat.  
Beaten down by time  
still eyelashes  
unable to dance.  
Dry wind  
whips the face  
and freezes the tears.  
they are blades  
without a Heart,  
pierced.  
Empty fountain  
lone heart  
pulsing on the rock.  
necrosis of time.

## My inner Child watches from afar

I spin around sharply  
I beg him to run away  
He doesn't deserve to see this.  
The childhood Puppet falls,  
Bloody footprints  
Screams.  
I hope he will be loved.

## The Void

the taste of the void  
has the shiver of vertigo  
while falling without guilt.  
it has the sound of the crash,  
it is a hole in the ground  
that receives the body  
and disowns time.  
it is brine that burns the eyes  
enough to blur the vision  
too little to erase a flower  
before it blooms.  
Resist.

## Breathe

I tremble seated  
in the front row.  
a child screams,  
terror and disgust  
end of the line.  
I fall from the train  
concrete and glare  
pain,  
I forget that it is summer.  
Silences and breaths  
sirens and candor.  
I ask for anesthesia  
to not suffer these sorrows  
but grey hairs ask me to live.  
I am still alive without love  
is that a good thing?

## The Shipwreck

days of storm,  
the waves hide dusks with no tomorrow.  
At home,  
salty candle  
Letters of ink.  
let the Words be shipwrecked  
let my Eyes speak.

## At the Hospital

At rest  
the pigeon of the blue building  
on a grate  
between narcissus and olive.  
Reflecting  
the comatose glass  
that white bracelet.  
One cannot enter yet,  
the child  
never loved.  
Ignores  
the abandoned phone,  
the teenager.  
Still alive.  
Feels  
the trampled leaf  
november,  
the footsteps.  
Outside  
couples dance.  
Who knows how it will be.

## Six Cranes

I see them.  
They are wreckage  
swept away by buildings.  
I know those who remain  
standing during earthquakes.  
They are noticed when one can see the sky  
but they are there to raise buildings.  
Silence in the construction site.  
Patient are they  
when they make the Future possible  
Crazy  
those who cannot see their lights  
except from above a plane  
at night.  
When will it pass?

## The Fog

aseptic days  
ice, fog and metal,  
empty streets  
of days gone by.  
Boxes full,  
the noise of packing tape,  
and they seem like gazes,  
the fragile flaps of cardboard,  
crossing each other.  
It will happen.

## Empty Benches

empty bench,  
muted songs  
streets with no destination  
no crossroads.  
another bench,  
unlaced shoes  
I notice couples  
for others no clamor.  
Termites devour the wood.  
a sound  
Warm on the ground.  
Unfortunately, I am awake.

## Absences

Subway off  
Passers-by, murals, songs  
closed doors  
streetlights reflected on the shutter.  
Only coffee and supplements  
I look away,  
I don't see but I feel it  
today's absence.  
Night sentinel.

## No Song

Whoever touches me is what resonates  
Piano without a pianist  
No melody  
I search for the echo between the notes  
Pianist without a piano  
I don't know the score  
No string vibrates.  
No heartbeat.  
Out of time?

## Swept Away

the wave overturns the clock,  
the sea does not stop  
it tears the grains of sand  
without eulogy.  
Without a part of you  
Wrong hour  
no longer on the beach  
but in the depths  
I find the clock again.  
Horde passed  
alas, youth obituary.  
It will work differently.

## The Wait

Ocean waves,  
Ancient sound.  
From the marina to infinity,  
a boy and a seagull at the pier.  
Authentic words,  
squawks of presence.  
Suddenly it is the hour of vespers.  
Someone walks, the other flies.  
Of ships the sea is only as far as one can see,  
time without end,  
living souls,  
in the wait for tomorrow.

## Between Dream and Reality

Walks  
the sleepwalker of thought  
and wakes at crossroads.  
Still dark  
Shivers and flashes  
atrocious thorns.  
He dreamed of an encounter that was real  
Bruises and idealizations  
When will it happen?
